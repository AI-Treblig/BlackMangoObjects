Welcome to BlackMangoObjects (“Black Mango Objects”, “we”, “our”, or “us”).

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you visit our website, place an order, contact us, or interact with our services.

Our website is powered by Shopify, which manages secure checkout, payment processing, and order management.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, contact us, or interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Gift messages or special instructions
  • Product customization details
  • Payment details processed securely through our payment partners

We do not store debit or credit card details on our servers.

Information Collected Automatically

When you browse our website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Website usage data
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring websites or sources
  • Cookies and tracking information

Information From Third Parties

We may receive information from trusted third-party services such as:

  • Payment gateways
  • Shipping and courier partners
  • Marketing and advertising platforms
  • Analytics providers
  • Social media platforms when you interact with our pages or advertisements

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Process payments securely
  • Personalize products or gifting requests
  • Improve website performance and customer experience
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized activity
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Send promotional emails or offers if you have opted in

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Users)

While we currently operate primarily within India, users outside India who access our services may still be protected under applicable privacy laws including GDPR.

For users in the EU or UK, we process personal information based on:

  • Consent
  • Contractual necessity
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Legal obligations

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Enable website functionality
  • Maintain shopping cart and checkout sessions
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze traffic and visitor behavior
  • Support marketing and advertising campaigns

You may disable cookies through your browser settings. Some parts of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5. Sharing of Information

We only share information with trusted service providers where required to operate our business, including:

  • Shopify for website infrastructure and order management
  • Razorpay and payment processors for secure transactions
  • Courier and logistics partners
  • Email and SMS communication providers
  • Security, analytics, and IT service providers
  • Government or legal authorities where required by law

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

6. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • SSL encryption (HTTPS)
  • Secure payment processing
  • Access-controlled systems
  • Limited employee access to customer data

While we take strong precautions, no online system is fully immune from security risks.

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information

  • Request deletion of your data

To make a privacy-related request, contact us using the details below.

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

After the required retention period, information is securely deleted or anonymized.

Our website and services are not directed toward individuals below the legal age required to make purchases under applicable law.
